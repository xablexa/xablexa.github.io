Character Mobility Demo

The data used for this demo is composed of 38 volumes of English language fiction. We plan to refine our approach and then process 100,000 novels. We track the following features:

  • Total Movements: the total number of movements that occur in the novel.
  • Movement Type: literal, hypothetical, negative hypothetical, figurative, or implied.
  • Movement Tense: past, concurrent, or future.
  • Movement Scale: small scale movements, medium scale movements, and large scale movements.
  • Characters: number of unique characters, number of instances of most occurring character (i.e. protagonist).
  • Origins: number of unique starting locations, most occurring starting location.
  • Destinations: number of unique destinations, most occurring starting destination.
  • Transportation: number of times transportation was specified, most common mode of transportation.
  • Quote: length of quote containing movement (# of characters).
