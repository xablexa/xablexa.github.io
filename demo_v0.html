Character Mobility Demo

Our project tracks every character movement that occurs in a novel, regardless of the scale of the movement. We have processed a few novels, but plan to process a sample of nearly 100,000 volumes of English language fiction. Our demo has 2 parts:

  • Interactive Table of Movements per Novel
  • Movement Annotator
